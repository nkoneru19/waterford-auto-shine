## Design Context

### Users
Car owners in Waterford Township, MI and surrounding Oakland County. They're browsing on their phone after googling "auto detailing near me" or clicking a Google Maps listing. They want to see if this place looks legit, what services are offered, and how to book. Decision happens in under 30 seconds.

### Brand Personality
Bold, kinetic, confident.

This is not a car wash. This is where your car goes to look like it just left the showroom. The site should make a $200 detail feel like a steal.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Bold & energetic — dynamic, eye-catching, makes people want to take action immediately
- **Theme**: Light backgrounds with one strong accent color, easily re-skinnable per client
- **References**: Body Shop By Shah (conversion-focused copy), TCL LP (premium motion), high-end automotive marketing
- **Anti-references**: Cheap Wix/Squarespace local biz templates, AI-generated generic (gradient text, glass cards, generic blue)
- **Must avoid**: Glassmorphism, gradient text, side-stripe borders, identical card grids, bounce/elastic easing

### Design Principles
1. **Conversion over decoration** — Every element drives toward a call or booking. If it doesn't convert, cut it.
2. **One unforgettable moment** — Each site has one hero moment that sticks (a dramatic before/after, a killer stat, a bold statement). Not twenty scattered micro-interactions.
3. **Bold typography carries the design** — Big, confident type does the heavy lifting. Not icons, not gradients, not effects.
4. **Motion is purposeful** — Scroll-triggered reveals and state changes only. No animation for animation's sake.
5. **Re-skinnable in 10 minutes** — Swap accent color + content config and the whole site transforms for a new client.
